About Open Range

Fair Proof-of-Work With Real Scarcity Baked in.

Open Range is inspired by Bitcoin’s Simplicity But Modernized For Today’s Miners and DeFi Users: Capped Supply, Predictable Emission, and a Fee Model That Doesn’t Forget The Small Players.

  • 50M Hard Cap – A Tight Supply Designed For Real Scarcity.
  • Accessible Mining – Tuned For GPU/CPU Rigs Instead of Industrial ASICs.
  • Aligned Economics – Fees Support Miners, Stakers, and a Permanent Burn.
  • No Central Treasury Drain – We Removed The Treasury Cut From Fees.
Why “Open Range”?

The Open Range is Where Anyone Can Ride – No Gates, No VIP Zones. That’s The Spirit of This Chain: No presale, No insiders, and an Emission Schedule That Lets New Miners Still Compete.

Mining Open Range

Built So Everyday Rigs Can Compete.

We’re Designing Open Range To Stay GPU/CPU-Friendly and Make it Tough For Huge ASIC Farms To Dominate. That Means Predictable Block Times, Fair Difficulty Adjustments, and No Weird “insider” Mining Advantage.

The Goal is Simple: If You Have a Halfway Decent Gaming PC or Second-Hand Mining Rig, You Can Participate Solo Or In a Mining Pool. No Need For a Miillion-Dollar Data Center.

  1. Download The Official Open Range Node + Miner (links will go here).
  2. Point Your Miner To The Official Pool or Run a Solo Node.
  3. Configure Intensity and Power To Match Your GPU/CPU.
  4. Start Hashing and Earning OPR Directly From Block Rewards and Fees.

Testnet First, Mainnet When it’s Ready.

Before Mainnet, We’ll Run an Open Testnet So Miners Can Dial in Their Hashrate, Try Different Rigs, and Help Us Tune Difficulty and Parameters For Real-World Conditions.
